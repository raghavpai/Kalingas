‘KALINGA’ – Scenic beauty meets divine food

A region whose reputation for scenic beauty and great hospitality is probably surpassed by one thing – divine food. A milestone in Bengaluru’s dining landscape, Kalingas brings the simple, subtle yet spicy culinary tradition of Odisha to the city.

Traditionally handed down from mother to daughter from one generation to the next, Odia cuisine is truly a labour of love. Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey that celebrates heritage, flavour and authenticity.

Kalingas reinvents the ‘Thali’

Available during lunch and dinner, the Kalingas’ Thali is created to give an authentic culinary experience of a traditional Odia meal. Enjoy a balance of flavours, textures and aromas served in a single platter.

À la Carte Dishes

From fiery Maachha‑Besara and Kashaa‑Maansha as part of our main course to the sublime Chhenaa Podo and Rabidi desserts, Kalingas is the closest you’ll get to Odisha while living in Bengaluru.

Snacks & Street Favourites

The snacks section offers a variety of authentic Odia snacks ranging from the mouth‑watering Dahibara Alu Dum and Kata Alu Singeda‑Ghuguni to our very own Odia Alu Chaat. Perfect for any time of day – drop by or order a quick bite.

Honest Food

Food as it should be: Kalingas’ focus is clear – honest food. Authentic. Great quality. Without the obligatory high price! Using less oil, coconut milk, rich curd and pancha‑phutana, our cuisine is slow cooked for maximum succulence and zing.

Culinary Journey

We are open from 7 AM to 11 PM every day. We take great pleasure in inviting you to take this culinary journey with us as we serve hearty meals and warm hospitality all day long.
