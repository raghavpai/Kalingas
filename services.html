Our Services

We deliver flavours and experiences

Home Delivery

Craving authentic Odia food at home? Our dedicated delivery team will bring your favourite dishes straight to your door. We offer **free delivery within a 2 km radius** of our restaurant, with no minimum order charge. Enjoy restaurant‑quality food in the comfort of your own home.

Corporate & Party Orders

Planning a celebration or corporate event? Let Kalingas cater your special occasion with our bespoke menus. We undertake bulk orders for birthdays, house‑warming ceremonies, office lunches and more. Contact us to discuss menu options, delivery times and customised packages.

Operating Hours

We are open daily from 7 AM to 11 PM. Delivery services are available during these hours. For large or corporate orders, please provide at least 24 hours’ notice.
